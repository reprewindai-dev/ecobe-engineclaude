# CO2 Router Design Partner Program

## Headline

Help shape the pre-execution control plane for compute.

## Summary

CO2 Router decides whether compute is allowed to run, where it should run, and under what environmental conditions before execution happens. We are inviting a small number of infra, platform, SRE, and data teams into a structured 3-month design partner pilot focused on one real workflow, fast activation, live proof, and a clear paid continuation path.

## Program shape

- 3 to 5 design partners maximum
- 3-month pilot
- 1 narrow scoped workflow per partner
- White-glove onboarding
- Biweekly feedback cadence
- Explicit graduation call before pilot end
- Explicit paid conversion path from day one

## Best-fit teams

- Infra, platform, SRE, or data teams with meaningful cloud, CI, or scheduled compute workloads
- Teams under pressure around emissions, compliance, workload placement, or infrastructure policy
- Teams with an internal champion and a practical commercial next step if value is proven

## Best-fit use cases

- GitHub Actions or CI routing
- Scheduled or batch workload routing
- Policy-gated high-compute jobs
- Limited-scope regional placement or delay decisions
- Environmental policy enforcement before execution

## What partners get

- Early access to CO2 Router in one real workflow
- Hands-on integration support
- Direct founder access
- Influence over roadmap inside the scoped pilot lane
- Preferred founding pricing if the pilot converts

## What we ask

- A 3-month pilot term
- One limited-scope workflow or workload family
- Biweekly product sessions
- Async feedback
- Practical integration cooperation
- Honest operational feedback
- Permission for anonymized results if the pilot succeeds

## Pilot structure

### Month 0

Confirm ICP fit, confirm the real workflow, sign the design partner agreement, define success metrics, create the CRM record, and schedule kickoff.

### Month 1

Run technical onboarding, connect the first scoped workflow, execute the first real policy decision, and reach first value.

### Month 2

Refine policies, proof surfaces, and workflow fit while collecting operational feedback and tracking usage.

### Month 3

Review outcomes, present the paid continuation plan, and convert, strictly extend, or close.

## Success metrics

- Accepted design partners
- Onboarding completion rate
- Time to first value
- Live workloads or policies governed
- Partner-sourced revenue
- Conversion to paid
- Expansion to additional workflows
- Case study, quote, or anonymized proof asset
